How it works

The exact flow, step by step.

End-to-end flow

  1. Connect TikTok. In MuTok you sign in with TikTok (OAuth). You grant scopes for basic profile info, video upload, and (optionally) video list for metrics.
  2. Plan. You add clips and tracks, approve snippets, and generate draft plans. MuTok suggests hooks, captions, and layouts based on your rules.
  3. Render. MuTok renders each plan to a video (or slide carousel) on your machine using FFmpeg. No video is sent to our servers.
  4. Upload as draft (inbox upload). You click “Upload drafts” in MuTok. The app uses TikTok’s “upload without posting” API: it sends the video to TikTok and you receive an inbox notification in the TikTok app.
  5. Complete in TikTok. You open the TikTok app, tap the inbox notification, finish editing (caption, privacy, etc.), and publish when you’re ready. MuTok does not publish to your feed.
  6. Learn (optional). If you use the video list scope, you can “Refresh metrics” in MuTok to pull view/like/comment/share data from TikTok and see leaderboards.

What MuTok does and does not do

  • MuTok uploads to TikTok inbox (not directly to the feed).
  • The user completes editing and posting inside TikTok.
  • MuTok does not automate likes, follows, comments, or messages.
  • MuTok only accesses data for the authenticated account that explicitly authorises access via TikTok Login.

MuTok does not auto-follow, auto-like, auto-comment, DM, or scrape. It does not publish directly to your feed; you complete posting inside TikTok.
