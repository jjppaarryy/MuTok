MuTok

A creator tool that helps artists and producers plan, render, and upload TikTok drafts.

What is MuTok?

MuTok is a creator's studio for artists and producers. It helps you plan music-first TikTok posts, render them locally, and upload them to TikTok as drafts. Drafts appear in your private tab in TikTok, where you can edit and set them to public when ready. MuTok does not publish directly to your feed.

Who it’s for

Creators who want to batch-plan and render content on their computer, then finish and publish from the TikTok app. Currently in limited beta for creators.

Plan & render

Plan posts with hooks, captions, and clips. Render video or slides locally with FFmpeg.

Upload to drafts

Upload rendered videos to TikTok as drafts. They appear in your private tab in TikTok, where you can edit and set them to public when ready.

Local-first

Your API keys and assets stay on your machine. We prioritize account security and data control.
